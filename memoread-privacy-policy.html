Privacy Policy

Memoread (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Memoread mobile application (the “App”). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

We collect minimal personal information necessary to provide and improve the App experience. This may include:

Your information is used only for:

Your data is securely stored using trusted cloud providers such as Supabase and OneSignal. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, including:

Data Retention and Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@memoread.app.

Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
